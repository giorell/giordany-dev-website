Contract Manager App

The Contract Manager app is a faster way to fill out short-term leases for rental units, intended for landlords in the Netherlands. Built for a client in Amsterdam, it cas the capability to keep track of addresses, tenants, and their respective contracts. It can generate a PDF that can be sent to both the landlord and the tenant, and a secondary PDF for the municipality.

Role

Designed and Architechted in a 6-week period.

Technologies

Coordinator-Pattern, Core-Data, Signature capability, camera capability.

Fantasy Football Six App

Built as a proof-of-concept, this fantasy football tracker is designed to allow up to 6 users to play as a management team for an American Football team during Football season. Technologies
